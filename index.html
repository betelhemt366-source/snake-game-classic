📐 CANVA GRAPHICS SPECIFICATION

🔥 MANDATORY POSTER TYPES

1. 🎮 Game Release Poster

Promote individual games. Must feature: Top Axumit Studios Logo, center Game Title (BIG/dominant), authentic gameplay-style dynamic illustration, short tagline (1 line max), "Play Now" CTA, and Web/Mobile platform tags.

2. 📢 Announcement Poster

Promote the overall arcade platform. Headline: "AXUMIT ARCADE IS LIVE". Subtext: "20+ Arcade Games. Fast. Simple. Addictive." featuring a visual grid of previews or abstract neon arcade vectors.

3. 🧑‍🎨 Designer Credit Poster

Mandatory. Credits go cleanly under the poster background or as a footer section: "Designed by: @username | Axumit Studios Creative Team". Never break visual hierarchy with giant credit text.

📐 CANVA SPEC & LAYOUT GRID

Pristine Dimensions: Main Vertical Aspect Ratio: 1080 × 1920 (9:16) for Mobile Stories/Reels. Square aspect ratio export: 1080 × 1080 (1:1) for static posts.
Golden Layout Grid (Strict): Top (15%) Zone: Axumit Branding Logo.
Middle (60%) Zone: Main Game Title + Rich Procedural Vector Visuals.
Bottom (25%) Zone: Direct Action CTA ("Play Now") and Designer Credits.
Official Palette: Background solid #0A0A0A. Primary accent Neon Cyan (#00FFFF). High-contrast supporting accent Neon Purple & Cyber Blue.

⚠️ COMMON DESIGN MISTAKES (REJECT IMMEDIATELY)

  • ❌ Too many discordant colors (Stick tightly to #00FFFF, Neon Purple, and dark gray).
  • ❌ Generic low-fidelity stock images (Use only authentic geometric gameplay visualizers).
  • ❌ Missing focal point or small, unreadable, low-contrast typography.
  • ❌ Over-designed pre-built Canva templates with generic template junk.
  • ❌ Inconsistent branding or missing mandatory designer signatures in footers.
